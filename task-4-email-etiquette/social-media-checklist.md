Responsible Social Media Use – Checklist for College Students

-----------------------------------------------------------------
Do’s:-

-----------------------------------------------------------------

Think before posting – Make sure your content is respectful and appropriate.

Protect your privacy – Use strong passwords and adjust privacy settings.

Verify information – Check facts before sharing news or claims.

Maintain a positive digital footprint – Share content that reflects your goals and values.

Engage respectfully – Be polite in comments and discussions, even during disagreements.

----------------------------------------------------------------------
Don’ts

----------------------------------------------------------------------
Don’t overshare personal information – Avoid posting sensitive details like address, phone number, or financial info.

Don’t spread misinformation – Never share unverified or fake news.

Don’t engage in cyberbullying or trolling – Harmful behavior can have serious consequences.

Don’t post in anger or frustration – Emotional posts can damage relationships and reputation.

Don’t neglect real-life responsibilities – Avoid excessive use that affects studies or health.
